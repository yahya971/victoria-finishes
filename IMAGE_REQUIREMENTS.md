## Image Guidelines

### Technical Specifications:
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Color Space**: RGB
- **Quality**: High resolution, professional photography
- **File Size**: Optimized for web (under 500KB each after optimization)

### Style Guidelines:
- **Lighting**: Natural, professional lighting
- **Composition**: Clean, uncluttered, focused on the product/finish
- **Color Palette**: Warm, sophisticated tones that complement the gold (#C5A059) brand color
- **Quality**: Professional photography only, no amateur or stock-looking images
- **Relevance**: Images should directly relate to Victoria Finishes' products and services

### Branding Consistency:
- Images should align with Victoria Finishes' premium positioning
- Showcase quality materials and professional installation
- Convey luxury, craftsmanship, and attention to detail
- Support the brand's message of "Premium Wallcovering & More"
